About Me

My name is Jonathan Patrick Gaghan. Originally from Mooresville, NC. Received three bachelors from UNCC in Finance, Accounting and Managament Information Systems. Previously was an infantryman in the army for 5 years while being stationed at Fort Bragg. Also owned a commericial landscaping company for about 7 years that served the communities from Statesville to the Charlotte area. Currently work at Bank of America for about a year and a half in the field of finance. Within this year and a half. I've worked in three different areas dealing with fixed income securities. These areas are Physical Settlements, Reorg Conversions and P&S Clearing (municipal and corporate side).

While working with Bank of America I've started to develop a different level of respect for what can be accomplished with coding. I learned my first object oriented form of coding Visual Basic For Applications. While this is a very basic code with limited functionality I managed to utilize it to help automate some of our processes and to help mitigate risk for 5 different areas within the firm. These applications save about 60 man hours weekly and one of them has proven to save the firm a minimum of two hundred thousand dollars a year.
